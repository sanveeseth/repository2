Space

Covid-19

Space, Space, full of heavenly bodies,
Some known some yet to explore.
The smell of dying stars and gases,
Is its distinct spoor.

Sun, Asteroid, Planets and Stars,
With my naked eyes, I want to see Mars.
O’ beautiful stars which give sky its shine,
Form constellations so divine!

The sun , the big bright ball,
Like a burning hall,
Gives the Moon, its brightness,
Without it the Earth would be lifeless.

The planets are a huge part of Solar System,
Orbiting the Sun is their lifelong mission.
Asteroid visible to scientist at night,
Make up an asteroid belt when they unite.

Meteoroids the one meter, rocky and metallic bodies in space ,
Also known as shooting star, superstition say, they decide our fate.

The Moon, Earth’s only natural satellite,
Neither have gravity nor a field of magnetite.

The surface of the Moon is rocky and uneven
The planets with most moons is Saturn.

Mercury is the smallest, second hottest and densest one,
Has a molten core and is closest to the sun.

Venus unlike others, spins clockwise on axis,
The day there, is longer than a year,
Has 90 times atmospheric pressure of the earth,
It is the hottest planet and could burn your ear.

Our mother earth, the only planet that supports life,
Has beautiful oceans, rivers and forests in rife.

Scientists are trying to find life on Mars
The red planet is named after Roman GOD of war.

Jupiter, the largest planet of all,
With 76 moons, is the fastest spinner of them all.
A great red spot has been surrounded for a long time,
Its magnetic field is just sublime.

Saturn, the beautiful planet with rings,
Mostly made of gases, can float on water like it has wings.

Unlike other planet it rotates on the side
Yes, it is Uranus with rings made of ice.

Neptune the farthest planet of Solar System,
Has 17 times mass of Earth and is little bigger than Uranus.

My dream is to be an astronaut,
Like Valentina cosmonaut.
The space is such a unique sight,
I observe it every night. 		Covid-19 is on every news station,
Ruining lives across the nation.

Over the world it spreads,
Because of which doctors can’t rest their heads.

This quarantine is so boring,
Inner creativity we all are exploring.

Can’t go to park to play,
Have to be at home whole day.

Oh! How much I miss my school, teachers and friends,
I pray to god that covid-19 soon comes to an end.

My dear friends, to prevent this virus easily,
Wash your hands more frequently.

Keep calm and be creative in this phase,
I request everyone to follow all rules and be safe.

Children don’t play outside, play indoors,
As this pandemic has no other cure.

Work from home don’t go out,
Social distancing should be followed through out.

A hero comes in many forms, this is very true,
To me, the heroes are the doctors and police, thankyou
